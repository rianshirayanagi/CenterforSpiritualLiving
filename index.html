A simple testimony 

Center for Spiritual Living gave me a place to explore, test and expand my beliefs. They never told me what to think, but simply invited me to learn new ways to more effectively think for myself.

They encourage me to always be myself, wherever I'm at on my journey.

I've made deep and lasting friendships with many people who I now consider closer than some of my own family. At CSL, people connect with one another soulfully, deeply. It's powerful, palpable, and enlightening. I longed for this kind of connection with others, but hadn't actually experienced in a church until I found these people.

Words aren't big enough to express what attending CSL has done for the betterment of my life.

I encourage anyone questioning their faith, beliefs, or ideals to attend. As long as you show up open-hearted and open-minded your experience is sure to be fulfilling and soul-filling.

Until this amazing community came into my life I hadn't even realized what I was missing. These people have become my greatest cheerleaders, community, support-system, family, true friends.

CSL Family Member- Raea S.
